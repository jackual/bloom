Bloom

This interactive work dynamically generates an audio-visual experience inspired by the spring season that corresponds with analysis of movement from a live camera feed. The ambient soundtrack accompanied with sounds of nature create a tranquil atmosphere inviting the audience to reflect on the season. As viewers step into the space, their presence breathes life into the responsive blooms on display that are born and die infinitely, symbolising the awakening of nature and the natural rhythms of rebirth and growth inherent to spring. This work also explores my personal experience of synaesthesia, where in which the auditory and visual senses are intrinsically intertwined, allowing synaesthetes to intuitively illustrate audio by seeing sound or score image by hearing colour.
